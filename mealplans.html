Why Create a Meal Plan?

Meal Planning can be a vital tool to healthy eating and a huge part of creating a balance in what we eat.

I did not say diet..

Creating a balance in your eating lifestyle does not mean dieting by any means. It means allowing you to organize your meals as a guide to balance your five food groups: Fruits, vegetables, grains, protein foods, dairy or fortified soy products every day.

It will make us aware of added sugars, saturated fat, and sodium.
