Why Create a Meal Plan?

Meal Planning can be a vital tool to healthy eating and a huge part of creating a balance in what we eat. Understanding our body type(BMI calculator). How much activity we spend, our age, sex, weight and height.(BMR calculator)

We can put together a plan for maintaining better health through our daily caloric consumption.
Basically we can adjust our coloric intake when we are less active and maintain fat(sedentary) growth. i.e working from home.

I did not say diet.. But. "A calorie deficit of 500 calories per day is effective for healthy and sustainable weight loss."" https://www.healthline.com/nutrition/

Creating a balance in your eating lifestyle does not mean dieting by any means. It means allowing you to organize your meals as a guide to balance your five food groups: Fruits, vegetables, grains, protein foods, dairy or fortified soy products every day.

It will make us aware of added sugars, saturated fat, and sodium.
